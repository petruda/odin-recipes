Polenta

Hungry and broke here is the solution.

One of the easiest and best tasting recipes, a polenta recipe straight form the pearl of Easter EUROPE, Romania

Ingridients

  • Polenta
  • Water
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Feta
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of Water for one Cup polenta
  2. Add polenta and mix till thick
  3. Add some butter and mix it in
  4. Put on a plate and add a Yogurt layer on top
  5. Grate some feta over it
  6. Fry an egg and put it on top
